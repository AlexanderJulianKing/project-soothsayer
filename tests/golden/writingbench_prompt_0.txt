In the eerily quiet Pattern Recognition Center, Willum the Sheepish Spy tapped his pen against a sheet of decrypted data, glaring at the blessedly dormant screens. Daylight had only just bled through the stained glass lamp on his desk—forgotten in the 1980s during a departmental bunker retrofit—casting an iridescent army of angels, lions, and half-melted monsters across his reports. The thing was, they were *mouthing* words.
